Overview

The Mercedes G-Wagon or G-Class is the ultimate SUV to a few, but to many, it's a status symbol.Manufuctured by Magna Steyr (Austria) and sold by Mercedes-Benz, its production begun in 1979.Originally designed as a military vehicle, it later became the luxury vehicle we know it to be today.In 2019, the latest model of the G-Wagon was designed with an improved interior,driver assitance and technology.
